Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book for bridal services?

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (November to February). Popular dates fill up fast — a 25% advance secures your slot.

Do you offer services at home or only at the studio?

Both! We have a fully equipped studio in Madurai and also offer on-site bridal services at your home, hotel, or marriage hall. Travel charges apply for venues outside city limits.

What is included in a bridal trial session?

A full trial covers the complete bridal makeup look plus the hair updo. We do this 2–4 weeks before your wedding, note every product used, and finalise all details so the wedding day is completely seamless.

Can I customise a package to fit my budget?

Absolutely. Our à la carte rate card lets you pick exactly what you need, and we're happy to build a custom package. Contact us with your requirements for a personalised quote.

How long does bridal makeup typically take?

Full bridal HD/airbrush makeup with hair takes 2.5–3.5 hours depending on complexity. Party or event makeup takes 45–90 minutes. We always recommend scheduling buffer time on your wedding morning.

Do you service clients outside Madurai?

Yes — we regularly travel to Dindigul, Sivaganga, Theni, Virudhunagar, and beyond. Travel and accommodation costs are quoted separately based on location. Contact us for outstation rates.
