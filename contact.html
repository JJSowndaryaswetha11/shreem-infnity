Frequently
Asked Questions

How early should I book for my wedding? +

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for bridal packages, especially during peak wedding season (October – March). This ensures availability and allows time for pre-bridal sessions and a trial run.

Do you provide on-site bridal services? +

Absolutely! We come to your venue — be it a hotel, home, or mandapam — anywhere in Madurai and surrounding districts. Our artists arrive fully equipped with professional lighting and all tools needed.

What is a trial session and is it necessary? +

A trial is a full run-through of your bridal look done weeks before your wedding. It lets you see your look, request changes, and go into your big day with complete confidence. We strongly recommend it for all brides.

Which makeup brands do you use? +

We exclusively use premium international brands including MAC, Huda Beauty, Charlotte Tilbury, Kryolan, and NARS. All products are dermatologically safe and suitable for all skin types and tones.

Do you handle all cultural wedding traditions? +

Yes! We specialise in Tamil, Telugu, Kerala, Christian, Muslim, North Indian, Gujarati, and Maharashtrian bridal looks. Our artists are deeply familiar with the specific aesthetic requirements of each tradition.

Can I customise my package? +

Every package is fully customisable. We understand each wedding is unique — you can add or remove services, combine packages, or build something entirely bespoke. Contact us to discuss a personalised quote.
